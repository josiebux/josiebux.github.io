it's not an easy puzzle!

Made Jan - Feb 2024

Read online here.

Sometimes it's easier to find the words to talk about something when you only have a few to choose from. I don't think I even sat down with the intention of making something about gender, I just started cutting out words that I liked and this is where I ended up.

Words chopped from a 2019 copy of Puzzler magazine (which I'd love to pretend was a pre-planned metaphor but it was just the nearest magazine I didn't mind cutting up), images from various public domain sources, feat misc coloured pencil additions.
