about me

I'm Josie. My pronouns are she/her.

I'm a creative dabbler, particularly interested in angels, the internet, identity, and most recently, digital collage.

I live in London with my wife and a cat called Phil Collins.

about this site

  1. I wanted somewhere to "drop" creative projects so that I actually finish them.
  2. I didn't really wanna make all my creative projects social-media friendly cause a.) social media is kind of awful now and b.) that's literally my day job.
  3. And it's been long enough since my Computer Science degree and brief coding career that I thought I would enjoy making a website again.

So here we are, welcome to josiebux.online I guess.
