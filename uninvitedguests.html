A riso print zine about a slow, weird end of the world.

An Incomplete Guide to Uninvited Guests is your concise, no-nonsense reference to the beings making themselves at home in our world. Intriguing for the casual hobbyist, and critically important for those in imminent physical danger, pick up your copy todayâ€”before they come back.

Collaged from historical artwork, sourced from a variety of public domain libraries (particularly the Rijksmuseum and this list of resources) and also one book of typefaces from 1880 that we found in my grandma's house.

Printed (with plenty of much-appreciated advice) by Luke at Yalla Riso.
